EchoNotes Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Core Commitment: EchoNotes respects and protects your privacy. We do not collect, upload, or analyze any of your personal data. All your data is securely stored on your device. This is an ADHD-friendly productivity app.

1. Data Collection

EchoNotes does not collect any personally identifiable information.

All content you create in the app, including:

  • Tasks and to-dos
  • Journals and notes
  • Ideas and inspirations
  • Goals
  • Habit tracking data
  • Other personal data

Is stored only on your device. We cannot access this data.

2. iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

  • Data is encrypted and transmitted via Apple's iCloud service
  • Synchronization is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure
  • We cannot access your iCloud data
  • You can disable this feature at any time in device settings

3. Third-Party Services

EchoNotes does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not:

  • Track your usage behavior
  • Collect device information
  • Display any advertisements
  • Share any data with third parties

4. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions, used only for these purposes:

  • Camera: Allows you to take photos to add to tasks or journals
  • Photo Library: Allows you to select existing photos to add to content
  • Face ID: Used to protect the private diary feature (optional)
  • Notifications: Used to send reminder notifications (optional)

All permissions can be disabled at any time in system settings.

5. Data Security

  • All data is stored in an encrypted database on your device
  • Private diary feature supports Face ID/Touch ID protection
  • No account registration required to use the app
  • You can export or delete all data at any time

6. Children's Privacy

EchoNotes is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Policy Updates

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page and update the date. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app.
